L.D. College Principal
Principal
Dr. Nilay N. Bhuptani brings a wealth of experience and a strong academic background to his role as Principal at LD College of Engineering. With an illustrious career spanning various leadership positions in academia and government, Dr. Bhuptani has played a pivotal role in shaping the landscape of technical education in Gujarat. His tenure as Principal at Vishvakarma Government Engineering College and his contributions as Joint Director of Technical Education for the Government of Gujarat underscore his commitment to excellence and innovation in higher education.
