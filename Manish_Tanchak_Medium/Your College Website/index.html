Welcome to Lalbhai Dalpatbhai College of Engineering

Dedicated to providing excellence in engineering education and fostering innovation and research.

Do you know?

L. D. College of Engineering endearingly known as L.D.C.E is Ahmedabad’s premier engineering college situated at the city’s heart and surrounded by elite organization like PRL, ATIRA, ISRO, IIM and CEPT. Starting in 1948 to impart quality higher education in various fields of engineering, it has seen unprecedented growth.

It is affiliated with Gujarat Technological University & administrated by Department of Technical Education, Government of Gujarat.

Vision

To contribute for sustainable development of nation through achieving excellence in technical education and research while facilitating transformation of students into responsible citizens and competent professionals.

Mission

  • To impart affordable and quality education in order to meet the needs of industries and achieve excellence in teaching-learning process.
  • To create a conducive research ambience that drives innovation and nurtures research-oriented scholars and outstanding professionals.
  • To collaborate with other academic & research institutes as well as industries in order to strengthen education and multidisciplinary research.
  • To practise and encourage high standards of professional ethics, transparency and accountability.

Lakshya Fest

Lakshya is a National level Edu-Tech festival of L.D. College of Engineering which is organized by ROBOCON LDCE every year since 2014. Every year a theme is decided and the main event is organized in accordance with the theme.

RepublicDay Celebration

Republic Day Celebration 2024 at LDCE
As the echoes of freedom resonates, L. D. College of Engineering welcomes you to join us for our annual Republic Day Celebration, a tradition upheld by the LDCE! The day will kick off with a flag hoisting ceremony, followed by a lively parade showcasing our commitment to national pride.
The Chief Guests for making the day more memorable are Dr. Viswas Amin, General Secretary, Indian Red Cross Society, Ahmedabad and Subedar B Jayesh , a distinguished Junior Commission Officer of Indian Army who served during the Kargil war.

Night Concert (Global Alumni Convention )

The Night Concert at the Global Alumni Convention of L. D. College of Engineering (LDCE) is a spectacular event that brings together alumni from across generations for an evening filled with music, nostalgia, and camaraderie.
This memorable night is designed to honor the strong bonds of the LDCE community, fostering connections and creating new memories.
